🌸 Glow Touch Booking

Glow Touch Booking Rules

  • Appointments must be booked at least 1 week in advance.
  • Each appointment lasts 1 hour 30 minutes.
  • Please arrive on time. Late arrivals may shorten your appointment.
  • If you need to cancel, please do so at least 24 hours before.
  • Glow Touch operates in Japan time (JST).

Opening Days & Times

  • Sunday: 2:00 PM – 3:30 PM (Only 1 appointment available)
  • Monday / Thursday / Saturday: 5:30 PM – 7:00 PM
  • Closed: Tuesdays, some Wednesdays, and Fridays

Notes Requirement

In the booking form notes, please tell us your preferences such as:

  • Soft / relaxing environment
  • Extra gentle treatment
  • Skin or comfort preferences
